About Me

I am a PhD Candidate at the Institute of Service Science, National Tsing Hua University. My research focuses on AI-enabled knowledge management systems, human-AI collaboration, and service science. I explore how generative AI and multi-agent systems can transform knowledge ecosystems and enhance organizational learning.

My work bridges theory and practice, combining action design research with advanced computational techniques including process mining, natural language processing, and agentic AI development. I have published papers at leading conferences such as HICSS and KMO, with research appearing in international journals.
