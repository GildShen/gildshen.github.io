About Me

I am a PhD Candidate at the Institute of Service Science, National Tsing Hua University. My work lies at the intersection of AI-enabled knowledge management and human-AI collaboration. I explore how generative AI and multi-agent systems can transform knowledge ecosystems, bridging advanced computational techniques with service science.

🧠 Current Research Focus

Pioneering the development of cooperative multi-agent knowledge service systems. Moving beyond simple assistants, my research aims to turn GenAI into intelligent allies that actively participate in organizational learning and complex decision-making processes.
